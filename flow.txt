This is a very basic flow of work done so far 

    A[Raw Sales Data] --> B{Clean Data: Remove Future Sales, Check Quality};
    B --> C{Analyze Product Contribution};
    C --> D[Focus Dataset: Top 10 Products];
    D --> E[Aggregate to Daily/Product Level];
    E[Daily Top 10 Sales Data] --> F{Outlier Capping (IQR) & Scaling};
    F --> G{Time-Series Feature Creation};
    G --> H{Time-Based Features: Day/Week/Quarter};
    H --> I[Lag Features (1, 7, 364 Days)];
    I --> J[Rolling Averages (7, 30 Days)];
    J --> K[Final Dataset];
    K --> L[Split Data: Train, Validation, Test];
    L --> M{Train: Simple Linear Regression (SLR)};
    M --> N{Result: SLR Baseline (18.82% MAPE)};
    N --> O{Train: XGBoost Regressor};
    O --> P{Result: XGBoost Performance (18.33% MAPE)};
